Welcome to Mengelmoes Boerdery

Mengelmoes Boerdery Pty (Ltd) is our startup farm, where we're passionate about cultivating premium-grade Moringa, Dragon Fruit, and Pomegranate. With a focus on sustainable practices and quality assurance, we're dedicated to delivering fresh, nutrient-rich produce. Our produce will supply our sister companies’ community projects, as well as both local and international markets, fostering healthier lifestyles worldwide.
